    Max Temperature

    This scatter plot demonstrates a correlation between Latitude and Temperatures with temperatures being higher as one approaches to the Equator. However the Equator (or 0 Latitude) is not the peak point of the trend. The highest temperatures were observed from 20 to 40 northern latitude.

    Equatorial zone is a zone with tropical humid climate where temperatures do not vary significantly during the year. Whilst the area of 20-40 northern latitude is a zone of arid and semi-arid climate (Saudi Arabia, Sudan, Iraq etc) with extremely hot weather in Summer.

    Our observation took part in Summer. Same observation performed during the year would move a peak point around the Equator shifting towards Southern Hemisphere during Winter.
